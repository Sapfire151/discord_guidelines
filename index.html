Adherence to these guidelines is mandatory for all members. Violations will result in immediate and decisive action, up to and including permanent removal from the platform and potential reporting to relevant authorities.

I. Prohibited Content and Conduct

1. Harassment and Bullying

Any form of **harassment**, including sustained bullying, sexual harassment, ban/block evasion, or coordinated server raids for the purpose of harassing members, is strictly prohibited. Refer to our Bullying, Harassment, and Threats Policy Explainer for further details.

2. Threats of Harm

Direct, indirect, or suggestive **threats** against any individual or group are expressly forbidden. Consult our Bullying, Harassment, and Threats Policy Explainer for comprehensive information.

3. Unauthorized Disclosure of Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

The sharing or threatening to share of an individual's **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)** without explicit consent is prohibited. This includes providing or facilitating doxxing services. Refer to our Doxxing Policy Explainer for a complete understanding.

4. Hate Speech and Hateful Conduct

The use of **hate speech**, hate symbols, imagery, or claims denying historical mass human atrocities is strictly prohibited. Hate speech is defined as any form of expression that attacks or promotes hatred or violence against them based on their protected characteristics. Review our Hateful Conduct Policy Explainer for additional context.

5. Violent Extremism

Organizing, promoting, or supporting **violent extremism**, including the coordination of violent acts; glorifying or promoting violence or the perpetrators of violent acts; and promoting conspiracy theories that could encourage or incite violence against others, is unequivocally forbidden. Consult our Violent Extremism and Violence and Graphic Content Policy Explainers for comprehensive details.

6. Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexualization

Soliciting, sharing, or attempting to distribute content that depicts, promotes, or normalizes **child sexual abuse**, or any content/conduct that sexualizes children (including real, manipulated, animated, or AI-generated media), is strictly prohibited. All instances of CSAM and grooming will be reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and may be forwarded to local law enforcement. Refer to our Teen and Child Safety Policy Explainer for more information.

7. Underage Sexual Conduct and Risky Behavior

Individuals under the age of 18 are prohibited from engaging in **sexual conduct** or any conduct that compromises their online or physical safety, including consensual sexual interactions between teens, as well as any encouragement or coordination of potentially risky behaviors, such as vigilantism. Spaces facilitating or encouraging dating among teens will be removed. Review our Teen and Child Safety Policy Explainer for further guidance.

8. Solicitation of Sexual Content from Minors

Soliciting sexual content from or engaging in any **sexual conduct with anyone under the age of 18** is strictly prohibited. All instances of CSAM and grooming will be reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and may be forwarded to local law enforcement. Refer to our Teen and Child Safety Policy Explainer for more information.

9. Distribution of Sexually Explicit Content to Minors

Sexually explicit content must not be made available to anyone under the age of 18. Users must be 18 years or older to engage with adult content on Discord. Server owners are required to apply an **age-restricted label** to any channels that contain sexually explicit content. Sexually explicit content is prohibited in any space that cannot be age-restricted, including in avatars, custom statuses or bios, server banners, server icons, invite splashes, emoji, and stickers. Consult our Sexual Content Policy Explainer for complete details.

10. Non-Consensual Adult Intimate Media

Sharing, distributing, or creating sexually explicit or sexually suggestive content of other people without the subject‚Äôs **consent** is forbidden, including the non-consensual distribution of intimate media. Refer to our Non-Consensual Adult Intimate Media Policy Explainer for more information.

11. Promotion of Suicide and Self-Harm

Content that glorifies, promotes, or normalizes **suicide or other acts of physical self-harm** (e.g., cutting, burning, starvation, eating disorders like anorexia/bulimia) is prohibited. Self-harm acts or threats used as a form of emotional manipulation or coercion are also forbidden. Review our Suicide and Self-Harm Policy Explainer for a comprehensive understanding.

12. Gore, Excessive Violence, and Animal Harm

The sharing of real media depicting **gore, excessive violence, or animal harm**, especially with the intention to harass or shock others, is strictly prohibited. Consult our Violence and Graphic Content Policy Explainer for additional information.

II. Respect for Discord Platform

13. Spam and Platform Manipulation

Sending unsolicited bulk messages (**spam**) to others and facilitating this activity (e.g., selling spambots, server ‚Äúraid‚Äù tools, account-creation tools, token generators, CAPTCHA-solving services, or other spam tools) is prohibited. Refer to our Platform Manipulation Policy Explainer for comprehensive details.

14. Self-Bots and User-Bots

The use of **self-bots or user-bots** is forbidden. Each account must be associated with a human, not a bot. Consult our Platform Manipulation Policy Explainer for further information.

15. Inauthentic Engagement

Engaging with our service in an **inauthentic way**, including artificially inflating server membership, manipulating engagement metrics, and selling artificial engagement services, is prohibited. Review our Platform Manipulation Policy Explainer for a full understanding.

16. Prohibited Sale of Discord Assets

The **sale or purchase of Discord assets**, including accounts, usernames, servers, server permissions, or custom server invite links, is strictly forbidden. Abuse of Discord's paid features or monetization opportunities are covered under our Paid Service Terms.

17. Misinformation

The sharing of false or misleading information (**misinformation**) that could lead to significant risk of physical or societal harm may not be shared on Discord. We may remove content if we reasonably believe its spread could result in damage to physical infrastructure, injury of others, obstruction of participation in civic processes, or the endangerment of public health. Refer to our Misinformation Policy Explainer for additional context.

18. Identity Misrepresentation

Misrepresenting your identity on Discord in a deceptive or harmful way. This includes creating fake profiles and attempts to **impersonate an individual, group, or organization**. Consult our Identity and Authenticity Policy Explainer for further details.

19. Evading Enforcement Actions

**Evading permanent Discord-level enforcement actions**, including creating new or using existing accounts or communities after being removed for violating our platform rules, is strictly forbidden. Review our Identity and Authenticity Policy Explainer for comprehensive information.

20. Account and System Security Compromise

Engaging in activities that could damage or compromise the **security of an account, network, or system**. This includes using deceptive techniques to trick others into revealing sensitive information (phishing), using malicious software (malware), and flooding a target with traffic in order to make a resource unavailable (denial-of-service attacks). Refer to our Deceptive Practices Policy Explainer for additional information.

21. Financial Scams

The use or attempt to use Discord to promote, coordinate, or execute **financial scams**. A financial scam is any intentionally deceptive act taken with the intent to receive an illegal, unethical, or otherwise dishonest gain. Consult our Deceptive Practices Policy Explainer for further details.

22. Fraudulent Profit Generation

Engaging in activities that **fraudulently generate a profit** at the expense of others. This includes facilitating, providing instructions for, and participating in fraud. We do not allow coordinated efforts to defraud businesses, price gouging, forgery, money laundering, counterfeit goods, or tools that facilitate illegal behavior. Review our Deceptive Practices Policy Explainer for a full understanding.

23. Abusive Reporting

Submitting **false, misleading, or abusive reports or requests** to Discord‚Äôs support teams. A report or request will be considered false or misleading if it contains demonstrably untrue information submitted with the intent to manipulate staff into taking a desired action. Refer to our Discord Support Abuse Policy Explainer for more information.

III. Compliance with Law

24. Intellectual Property and Other Rights Violations

Sharing content that violates anyone's **intellectual property or other rights**. This includes sharing or selling game cheats or hacks. Consult our Unauthorized Copyright Access Policy and Copyright & Intellectual Property Policy for additional information on copyright complaints.

25. Regulated or Dangerous Goods

Organizing, promoting, or engaging in the selling or facilitation of sales of **regulated or potentially dangerous goods**. Regulated goods have laws in place that restrict the purchase, sale, trade, or ownership of the goods. Dangerous goods have a reasonable potential to cause real-world, physical harm to individuals. Refer to our Dangerous and Regulated Goods Policy Explainer for further details.

26. Illegal Gambling

Coordinating or participating in **illegal gambling**. Users are responsible for complying with applicable gambling laws and regulation. Review our Gambling Policy Explainer for more information.

27. Other Illegal Activity

Organizing, promoting, or engaging in any other **illegal activity**, such as human trafficking or sexual solicitation. Consult our Human Trafficking and Sexual Solicitation Policy Explainers for more.

29. Confidentiality
